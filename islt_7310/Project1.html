My Projects: Video Editing

Video Editing

This video highlights my skills as a video editor. I edited this video in Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects. Some Premiere Pro editing skills included in this video include fade-ins and fade-outs, text movement, speed alteration, continuity editing, appropriate pacing, and visual cuts lined up with audio cues.


Some Adobe After Effect skills featured include use of fractal noise, masking, blur effects, and write-on text animation. This video was created as a final project in COMMUN 4880 at the University of Missouri. I enjoy editing videos that have a strong audio presence because I like to edit on the beat and let the visuals reflect what you are listening to.


Not only did this project teach me new editing skills, but I also learned the importance of storyboarding and pre-production. I shot all the footage featured in the video, and I had several instances where I had to reshoot scenes because the footage did not fit together. Overall, it was a great experience to make this music video, and I look forward to the chance for me to make a video like this again!
