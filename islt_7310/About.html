About Me: Elizabeth Schlaker

Education Overview

Currently I am a junior at the University of Missouri. I will graduate December 2022 with a Bachelor of Art in Communication along with minors in information technology, film studies, and Spanish. I will also graduate with an information systems certificate, media technology and design certificate, multicultural certificate, and honors college certificate.


For the past two years I have worked as a Resident Advisor with the Department of Residential Life, and this past semester I began working as a marketing intern at a local bond company. On campus I serve on the executive board of Mizzou’s communication honors society, Lambda Pi Eta, as secretary. I am also treasurer of Shot Reverse Shot, a student run video production organization. For six semesters I have been a member of Little Sisters of the Gold Rose, a service sorority.
