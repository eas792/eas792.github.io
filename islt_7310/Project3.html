My Projects: Filmmaking

Filmmaking

This short film highlights my skills on set as a production assistant, slate supervisor, and grip. This film was produced, shot, and edited by a group of classmates for FILM_VS 3550 using a C100 camera, quasar lights, and a zoom field recorder.


This film was difficult to make because we shot both at night and during the day. When we shot during the day, we had to make the inside of the house appear as though it was nighttime. When we shot outside at night, we had to light the scene well enough to capture clear images.


I learned so much about how to properly light a scene, and since it was a small crew I got familiar with all the on-set departments.
