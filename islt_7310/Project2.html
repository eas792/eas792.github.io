My Projects: Motion Graphic Effects

Motion Graphic Effects

This video highlights my skills as a motion graphic effects artist. All the effects showcased here were done in Adobe After Effects for INFOTC 3640 class projects.


The first clip shows usage of advanced lightening and lens flares.


The second video uses masking, green screen keylight, lower third graphics creation, and composite layers.


The third video utilizes reverse motion tracking, animated masks, the liquify tool, and adjustment layers.


The fourth video highlights motion blur.


Finally, the last video uses fractal noise, compositing, colorama, shatter, and circle effects. This class was the first time I had ever worked with After Effects, so there was a learning curve for me to figure out how the program worked. By the end of the class, I was very impressed with my skills and the different features of After Effects that I was able to master, such as key framing and compositing.
